Asian Chicken with Ginger and Bok Choy
Served over brown basmatic rice, this is dish is a meal-in-one, that is quick and easy to prepare, and more importantly, has great flavor!
Prep and Cook Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 2 TBS low sodium chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup scallions, chopped
  • 1-1/2 tsp minced ginger
  • 3 oz chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2-1/2 cups bok choy, chopped
  • 1/2 TBS rice vinegar
  • soy sauce, salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Heat broth in a stainless steel skillet.
  2. When broth begins to steam, add scallions and Healthy Saute for 2 minutes.
  3. Add ginger and continue to sauté for another minute.
  4. Add chicken and continue to sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add shiitake mushrooms and bok choy. Continue to sauté for another 3-4 minutes, and then add rice vinegar and season with salt, pepper, and soy sauce to taste.

Serve with 1 cup brown rice.

Serves 1
